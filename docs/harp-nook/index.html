The Harp Nook

Your donation based tailored home harp experience

The last couple of weeks have been really tough: social distancing is not part of human nature and we are forced to spend most of our time at home.

We are missing more than ever going out with our friends, family and partner, not being able to create and share experiences that last in our memories.

I would like to create with your help something new: a professional harp experience tailored and shaped just for you to share precious time with your loved ones.

Contact me About me

We can decide on a day and time that suits all the people you would like to meet or surprise.
I will then share you a link that will work for around 60 minutes and...your unique special experience is ready to start!
At the end of our call, you will be given a PayPal link to make me a donation..hoping you enjoyed the experience!!

Experiences

Date Night
Share an intimate dinner with your other half, listening to romantic harp music in the background (ask in advance for any specific songs that might mean something special to you)
Grab a Pint
Join your friends in a chilled “home/pub” performance, enjoying classical and pop music while having a drink and chat with your buddies
Quiz Night
Take part to a little game with your family and/or friends, guessing the most famous film music soundtracks
Nap Time
Make your children listen to harp lullabies and songs, helping them (and parents!) handling days without school with a soothing and calming effect during their play or nap time
Time with Granny and Grandad
Show your elder relatives that you are thinking of them even if you can’t meet at the moment: give them a call and let them enjoy some classical music to lift the fear and anxieties for a while
Company Get Together
Grab a coffee, chat with your collegues and listen to soothing harp melodies.
Tell me your personalised ideas, I will be glad to arrange something different together!
